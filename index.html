    Zuke Platformer - a 2D game developed in LOVE2D engine using Lua

    While delving into Lua programming and fueled by a passion for game development, I discovered Love2D, an engine that piqued my interest. Within this endeavor, I utilized a physics library known as Windfield to manage body collisions effectively.     We Defeat Nations

    I am currently working on this game, a true first-person shooter where you can visualize the complete (or almost complete) body of your character, much like in real life. I have been focusing extensively on 3C (Character, Camera, and Controls) so that the character aligns closely with the final vision. We now have the character's movement and rotation (Turn in place) functioning, as showcased in the videos below. Full-body IK has also been implemented, applying IK to the hands and feet of the character.

    I have started working on the game's UI, as evident in the video. Currently, only the Health aspect is programmed, with the next step being the completion of the game's Overlay.
